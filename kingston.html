Alpha

The Alpha Institute is a

school that has produced

some of Jamaica’s

greatest music legends

including trombonist Don

Drummond. Visitors can

tour the grounds and

play Reggae with Alpha

Alumni. Family-friendly

The final resting place of

the musician who helped

Jamaican contemporary

music and created a legacy of work that would be drawn upon later by reggae and ska artists

mementoes. Family-friendly
